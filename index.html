It is aimed both for programmers who know nothing about Node.js and for Node.js programmers who want to learn new things. You can read more at the <a href="http://nodeschool.io" target="_blank">official NodeSchool website</a>. NodeSchool Lodz is an open initiative. It was started by <a href="http://goodylabs.com" target="_blank">goodylabs</a> and has received support from companies like GFT or Comarch. If you want to help, contact Krzysztof (k.zbicinski at gmail.com). ## Next meeting The next meeting will take part in the first half of May: **When:** 13th May 2017, 10:00 - 16:00. **Where:** Comarch office in <a href="https://goo.gl/maps/gZW7F3vbkom" target="_blank">Jaracza Street 76, Lodz</a>. **Price:** free, however <a href="https://ti.to/nodeschool-lodz/workshop-7" target="_blank">registration is required</a> (registration starts on 29th April, 12:00 a.m. CEST). **Main topic:** Progressive Web Apps. **We are looking for mentors!** Don't worry, it's as easy as coming to the workshops and answering questions from participants.
